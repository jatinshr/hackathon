About Us

We are a non-profit social welfare platform. Founded by Anjanay Raina, Jatin Kumar Sharma and Bhavya Jain in the year 2021, We are your bridge between social welfare socities and NGO's.This platform continues to work for the elimination of child labor, empowerment of women , inclusion of women in STEM fields and uplifting the needy and the poor.We have partrned with many of the NGO's .You can either directly reach them using the links given below or you can post a comment in the global chat and we will help you to recognize what can be done in order to resolve your problem ,and get your story to the world.

Our Working

When you post your story to us , you will recieve a option of giving us your mail or phone number , this is done so we can reach you reagrding your problem if we think your problem is legitimate.Remember , your email is just with us and your anonymity will remain absolute no matter what ,the requirement for your mail is just for the team at Help Karo and the user to have a medium of communcation. We will then ask for some proof regarding your claims and if those proofs are found to be satisfactory , we will dispatch our inspection team to get a idea of the situation. If the claims are found to be true we direcly engage with the NGO's and welfare societies to help resolve the issue in your area.
